Strogonoff

Description

A Simple recipe that uses very few dishes, it only takes 45 minutes to prepare and yields 4 portions

Ingredients

  • 500 grams rump cut into strips
  • 1 quarter cup butter
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • 1 pinch black pepper
  • 1 chopped pelled tomato
  • 300 grams table cream
  • salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a pan, heat 1 quarter of butter
  2. Place the rump in strips in the pan with salt to taste
  3. add black pepper to taste
  4. Let the meat brown for 2 minutes without stirring so it doesn't lose its liquid
  5. Add 1 tablespoon of mustard and 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  6. Then add 1 chopped tomato without the skin to the sauté
  7. Also add 1 chopped onion
  8. Mix and let it sauté well
  9. Add 300 grams of cream, mix and let it cook for another 2 minutes
