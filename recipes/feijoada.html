Feijoada Brasileira

Description

It consists of a bean stew, usually with meat, and almost always accompanied by rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pig's ear cut into small pieces
  • 2 pig's feet cut into small pieces
  • 1/2kg of dried meat cut into small pieces
  • 1/2kg of pork ribs cut into small pieces
  • 1/2kg of black beans
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 300 grams of smoked Calabrian sausage cut into thick slices
  • 300 grams of paio sausage cut into thick slices
  • 400 grams of pork loin cut into cubes
  • 1 medium orange without the peel
  • 2 cubes of bacon
  • 2 liters of boiling water

Steps

  1. The day before, put the pig's ears and feet in a bowl and cover them with cold water
  2. In another bowl, put the dried meat and pork ribs and cover them with cold water to desalt
  3. The next day, put the beans in a bowl, cover with water and set aside for 1 hour
  4. Drain the pigs' ears and feet and place them in a pressure cooker covered with cold water
  5. Cover the pot and cook for 15 minutes from the start of the pressure
  6. Remove from the heat, wait for all the steam to escape and open the pot
  7. Drain and set aside
  8. Drain the dried meat and ribs and place them in the pressure cooker covered with cold water
  9. Cover and cook for 15 minutes from the start of the pressure
  10. Remove from the heat, wait for all the steam to escape and open the cooker
  11. Drain the meat and set aside
  12. In the same pan, heat the oil and brown the onion
  13. Add the sausage, paio and loin and saut√© for another 3 minutes
  14. Add the drained beans, the reserved pre-cooked meat and the orange
  15. Add the bacon cubes and boiling water
  16. Cover and cook for 15 minutes from the time the pressure starts
  17. Remove from the heat, wait for all the steam to escape and open the pan
  18. Remove the orange and put it back on the heat
  19. Cook over a low heat, with the pan partially covered (without the pressure), for another 30 minutes or until the broth thickens and the ingredients are cooked
  20. Transfer to a serving dish and serve
